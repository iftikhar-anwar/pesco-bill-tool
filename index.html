What is PESCO?

Peshawar Electric Supply Company (PESCO) distributes electricity to over 2.6 million consumers within Peshawar and surrounding districts of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

What is PITC?

Power Information Technology Company (PITC) creates and maintains the centralized online billing software used by all DISCOs in Pakistan, including PESCO.

Common Problems & Solutions

❌ Bill not found
Ensure the 14-digit reference number is correct. If the bill was just issued, it may take 24-48 hours to appear online.
❌ Invalid Reference Number
Double-check for typos. The reference number must be exactly 14 digits with no alphabets.
⚠️ Site not opening
The PITC server might be down due to heavy traffic. Try again after a few minutes.

Where to find Reference Number?

Your Reference Number is a 14-digit code located in the top-left or top-right section of your electricity bill.

BATCH NO: 02
SUB DIV: 12345
REF NO: 1234 567 890 1234

You only need to enter the numbers. Do not include the 'R' or 'U' found at the end of some identifiers.
